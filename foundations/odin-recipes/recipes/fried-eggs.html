Fried Eggs

Description

A fried egg is a cooked dish made from one or more eggs which are removed from their shells and placed into a frying pan and fried.

Ingredients

  • Egg(s)
  • Butter
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Add butter to heating pan.
  2. Crack egg into the pan.
  3. Add salt.
  4. Properly turn the egg so it fries on both sides.
  5. Stop when it's fried enough as desired.
