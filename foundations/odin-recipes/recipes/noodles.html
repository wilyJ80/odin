Noodles

Description

Instant noodles, or instant ramen, is a type of food consisting of noodles sold in a precooked and dried block with flavoring powder and/or seasoning oil.

Ingredients

  • Noodle Block
  • Water
  • Flavoring Powder

Steps

  1. Fill pan with water.
  2. Start heating.
  3. Add noodle block.
  4. Add flavouring powder.
  5. Wait until it is good enough.
